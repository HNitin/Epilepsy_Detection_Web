EPILEPSY DETECTION SYSTEM

Epilepsy is a neurological disorder characterized by recurrent seizures. Seizures are abnormal, excessive, and synchronous electrical activity in the brain, which can lead to a wide range of symptoms depending on the area of the brain affected. These symptoms can vary from brief lapses of attention or muscle jerks to severe convulsions and loss of consciousness.
